Our Story

Praise Revival Sanctuary International (PRSI) was founded with a vision to revive the nations through praise and worship. Our journey began in Accra, Ghana, where we established our first sanctuary to create a space for people to experience God's presence through worship.

Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant community of believers committed to transforming lives through the power of God's Word, prayer, and worship. Our ministry extends beyond the walls of our sanctuary through our radio station, Revive Radio, which reaches thousands of listeners with uplifting spiritual content.

We believe in creating an inclusive environment where everyone, regardless of background, can experience God's love and grace. Our doors are open to all who seek spiritual growth and a deeper relationship with God.

Our mission is to create an atmosphere where people can encounter God, experience transformation, and be equipped to fulfill their divine purpose.

What We Believe

At Praise Revival Sanctuary International, our beliefs are rooted in the Word of God. We believe:

  • In one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • In the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, death on the cross, bodily resurrection, ascension to the right hand of the Father, and His personal return in power and glory.
  • That the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and life.
  • In the present ministry of the Holy Spirit by whose indwelling the Christian is enabled to live a godly life.
  • In the resurrection of both the saved and the lost; those who are saved unto the resurrection of life and those who are lost unto the resurrection of damnation.
  • In the spiritual unity of believers in our Lord Jesus Christ.
  • In the power of praise and worship as a spiritual weapon and a means to experience God's presence.
